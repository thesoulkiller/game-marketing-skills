# Game Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-02-15*

## Game Overview
**Title:** Iron Vanguard: Tactical Warfare
**Genre / Sub-genre:** Mid-core / Tactical RPG
**Platform targets:** iOS (App Store) + Android (Google Play)
**Core loop:** Deploy squads → execute turn-based tactical missions (10–20 min) → collect gear and hero shards → upgrade roster → tackle harder difficulty tiers
**Art style:** Realistic with stylized character portraits; dark military aesthetic
**Setting / theme:** Near-future dystopian warfare; mercenary squads across global conflict zones
**One-line pitch:** The squad tactics game that rewards real strategy — no pay-to-win, just outthink your enemy.

## Target Player
**Primary age range:** 18–35
**Player type:** mid-core
**Gender skew:** Male-skewing (~75% M / 25% F estimated)
**Key motivations:**
- Deep tactical challenge and squad customization
- Hero/gear collection and progression
- Competing on leaderboards and guild war rankings
- Strategy mastery and "big brain" moments
**Session habits:** 15–25 min sessions, 2–3x per day; peak play evenings and weekends
**Device profile:** Mix of iOS flagship and Android mid-range; iOS slightly dominant in Tier 1 markets
**Priority markets:** US, Germany, UK
**Player archetypes:**
| Archetype | Who they are | Why they play |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| The Tactician | 25–34M, strategy game veteran, plays on iPad | Loves outsmarting AI and PvP opponents with clever builds |
| The Collector | 22–30M/F, RPG fan, plays on commute | Driven by completing hero rosters and maxing gear sets |
| The Guild Leader | 28–38M, competitive, plays daily | Wants to lead guild wars and dominate rankings |

## Monetization Model
**Model type:** F2P + IAP
**IAP categories:**
- Hero and gear gacha / loot boxes
- Battle pass (seasonal, 30-day)
- Progression boosters (energy refills, XP accelerators)
- Cosmetics (squad skins, base themes)
- Starter pack and value bundles
**Key price points:** starter pack $4.99 · popular bundle $19.99 · top IAP $99.99
**Ads monetization:** Rewarded video only (optional energy refills); no interstitial or banner
**Monetization philosophy:** Balanced — whale-accessible gacha with broad battle pass payer base; cosmetics provide non-pay-to-win spending lane
