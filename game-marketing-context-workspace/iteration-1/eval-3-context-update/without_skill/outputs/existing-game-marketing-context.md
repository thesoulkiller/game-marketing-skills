# Game Marketing Context

## Product Overview

- **Game Name**: Ironbound Chronicles
- **Genre**: Mid-core Tactical RPG
- **Platform**: iOS / Android
- **Monetization**: Free-to-play (IAP + Battle Pass)
- **Stage**: Soft launch → Global launch prep

## Target Audience

- **Age Range**: 18–35
- **Gender**: Primarily male, some female
- **Interests**: Strategy games, RPGs, fantasy, esports, anime
- **Markets**: US (primary), DE (secondary), UK (secondary)
- **Psychographic**: Competitive players who enjoy min-maxing, collection mechanics, and guild-based social play

## Creative Strategy

- **Hero hook**: Tactical depth — "It's chess with dragons"
- **Formats**: 15s video (feed), 30s video (playable pre-roll), static banners
- **Tone**: Gritty fantasy, high-stakes battles, squad-building moments
- **Top-performing concepts (hypothesis)**:
  - Underdog comeback gameplay clips
  - Hero unlock / collection reveals
  - Guild war highlights

## UA Channels

- Meta Ads (primary)
- Google UAC (secondary)
- Apple Search Ads (iOS primary)
- Influencer / TikTok (test budget)

## Messaging Pillars

1. Tactical mastery — outsmart opponents, not outspend
2. Deep roster — hundreds of heroes to collect and synergize
3. Guild wars — compete with friends for global rankings
4. Regular content cadence — new heroes, events, seasons

## Localization Notes

- **DE**: Strong preference for strategy depth messaging; avoid pay-to-win perception
- **UK**: Humor-friendly; lean into competitive rivalry narratives
- **US**: Broad — balance action hooks with depth for upper funnel
