## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Players love | Falls short | Our edge |
|------------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Royal Match (Dream Games) | Extremely polished puzzle loop, low friction, strong narrative framing with king character | City-building is surface-level decoration only; limited strategic depth | Deeper kingdom-building layer gives progression-hungry players more to invest in |
| Merge Dragons / Merge Magic (Gram Games) | Satisfying merge mechanic, nature/fantasy theme, cozy pacing | Merge loop can feel repetitive; limited direct skill expression | Active puzzle battles give players direct agency and skill expression each session |
| Township (Playrix) | Strong city-building and farming loop, social features, high polish | Puzzle component (match-3) feels disconnected from city building | Tighter integration between puzzle outcome and kingdom advancement creates cohesion |

**Most convertible competitor audience:** Royal Match players who want more depth; Township players who want more active gameplay during build timers.
