# Game Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-03-15*

## Game Overview
**Title:** Iron Vanguard: Tactical Command
**Genre / Sub-genre:** Mid-core strategy / tactical RPG
**Platform targets:** iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play)
**Core loop:** Player deploys squad on tactical grid → defeats enemy encounter → earns XP + gear → upgrades heroes and unlocks new abilities, repeating every 15–25 minutes.
**Art style:** Semi-realistic, dark fantasy with stylized unit animations
**Setting / theme:** Gritty medieval fantasy with strategic hex-grid combat
**One-line pitch:** Deep tactical RPG with the accessibility of a mobile strategy game — build your squad, dominate the battlefield.

## Target Player
**Primary age range:** 18–35
**Player type:** Mid-core
**Gender skew:** Primarily male
**Key motivations:**
- Strategic depth and squad optimization
- Progression and collection (hero roster building)
- Competitive ranking and leaderboards
- Tactical mastery and skill expression

**Session habits:** 15–30 min sessions, 2–3x per day, primarily evenings and weekends
**Device profile:** Mix of iOS flagship and Android mid-to-high range
**Priority markets:** US, Germany, UK
**Player archetypes:**
| Archetype | Who they are | Why they play |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| The Tactician | 25–35M, strategy game fan, plays PC/console too | Loves optimizing squad compositions and mastering the combat system |
| The Collector | 20–30M, gacha-familiar, enjoys meta-game | Driven by hero collection, tier lists, and progression systems |
| The Competitor | 22–35M, rank-focused, plays daily | Motivated by PvP ladder, guild wars, and seasonal rankings |

## Monetization Model
**Model type:** F2P + IAP
**IAP categories:**
- Gacha / hero summon banners
- Battle pass (seasonal)
- Progression boosters (XP, stamina refills)
- Cosmetics (unit skins, commander frames)
- Starter and value bundles

**Key price points:** Starter pack $4.99 · Popular bundle $19.99 · Top IAP $99.99
**Ads monetization:** Rewarded video only (optional for stamina refills and retry tokens)
**Monetization philosophy:** Whale-friendly — high ARPPU from dedicated spenders; free players progress but spenders get meaningful edge in collection speed and PvP ranking

## Competitive Landscape
| Competitor | Players love | Falls short | Our edge |
|------------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Raid: Shadow Legends | Deep roster, production value, lots of content | Auto-battle dependency, pay-to-win perception | More skill-expression in combat; tactical decisions matter every turn |
| AFK Arena | Low friction, idle progression, art style | Lacks tactical depth, too passive | Active combat with real decisions; rewards skill not just spend |
| King's Raid | Deep hero lore, loyal community | Dated UI, aging player base | Modern UX, fresh IP, better onboarding |

**Most convertible competitor audience:** Raid: Shadow Legends lapsed players who want more tactical engagement
